Our Vision for a Connected Africa

Sustainably improving living conditions by reducing the digital and energy divide.

The RMS Impact Project

RMS Impact is an initiative of RMS International Group designed to provide an integrated solution to the challenges of accessing essential services in rural Africa. By combining access to the Internet, electricity, and drinking water, we create an ecosystem conducive to economic and social development.

Our approach is based on a Public-Private Partnership (PPP) model, where we work closely with governments to obtain logistical support and tax exemptions, while we mobilize investments and manage the design, installation, and maintenance of the infrastructure.

The Concrete Impacts We Achieve for Communities

✅ Economic Development

Stimulating local economic activities and creating over 500 direct and 1,000 indirect jobs.

✅ Improved Living Conditions

Increased access to education and health through e-learning and telemedicine, and reduction of waterborne diseases through access to drinking water.

✅ Inequality Reduction

Connecting isolated populations to essential services to reduce the digital and social divide.

✅ Environmental Sustainability

Prioritizing the use of renewable energies (solar) to reduce CO₂ emissions and fuel costs.

Why Invest in RMS Impact?

A unique opportunity to combine transformative social impact with large-scale financial viability.

A Pan-African Investment Opportunity

The RMS Impact project is structured for a large-scale, financially sustainable deployment. With a solid economic model based on paid essential services, we project a return on investment per project within 5 to 7 years.

We are seeking financial and institutional partners to support this vision and accelerate deployment across the continent.

Global Deployment Funding Needs

Based on our target of **~63,000 sites** to reach 30% of the identified potential market:

  • Total estimated budget: $7.5 - $10.7 billion USD.
  • Financing model: A mix of concessional loans, grants, private investments, and Public-Private Partnerships (PPPs).

Annual Revenue Projections (at full capacity)

  • Total estimated annual revenue: $1.8 - $3.1 billion USD.
  • Revenue sources: Sale of energy, water, and Internet services to local communities.

Republic of Ghana

Context and Opportunities

Ghana has one of the highest electrification rates in sub-Saharan Africa, yet pockets of energy poverty persist in rural areas and on the islands of Lake Volta. The country is also a leader in digital transformation, creating strong demand for reliable connectivity to support its dynamic economy.

Potential Intervention Zones

The northern regions (Northern, Savannah) as well as island and lakeside communities represent niche markets where RMS Impact's off-grid solutions can provide high added value, complementing the national grid.

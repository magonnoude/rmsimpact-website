Gabonese Republic

Context and Opportunities

Gabon is characterized by a high GDP per capita and a high rate of urbanization. However, significant inequalities persist, and rural and forested areas lack infrastructure. The national strategy to diversify the economy, "Green Gabon," focused on ecotourism and sustainable forest management, creates a demand for clean and reliable energy and communication solutions.

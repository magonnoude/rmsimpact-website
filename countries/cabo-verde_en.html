Republic of Cabo Verde

Context and Opportunities

Cabo Verde, a stable and developing archipelago, has a high dependence on imported fossil fuels and great potential for renewable energy (solar and wind). With tourism as a pillar of its economy, the demand for high-performance internet connectivity and green energy is very strong. The island geography makes RMS Impact's decentralized solutions particularly relevant.

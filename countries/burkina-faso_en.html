Burkina Faso

Context and Opportunities

Burkina Faso faces significant security and climate challenges that increase energy poverty and the isolation of rural communities. In this context, decentralized and resilient solutions like those offered by RMS Impact are essential to maintain basic services, support displaced populations, and strengthen local stability.

Potential Intervention Zones

The Centre-Nord, Sahel, and Est regions are priority areas where access to services is critical. The integrated approach (Water, Energy, Internet) would create resilience hubs for health centers, schools, and local economic activities.

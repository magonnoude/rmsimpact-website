M. Modeste AGONNOUDE

Initiator & President of RMS Impact

(France, Bénin)

Modeste Agonnoudé is the founder and President of RMS Impact. Engineer and strategist, he spent nearly a decade at the heart of the continent's energy transformation as Chief Information Officer (CIO) and Chief of Staff to the CEO of Engie Africa. It was there that he led major electrification projects (mini-grids, solar kits) that forged his conviction: the future of Africa will be built by bringing energy, water, and Internet to all. Holding an MBA from the École des Ponts Business School and a certificate from MIT, he combines technical expertise and strategic vision to deploy high-impact solutions.
